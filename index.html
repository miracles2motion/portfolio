About Me

I'm Aigbomian Miracle, a multidisciplinary designer and motion artist based in Nigeria, specializing in creating compelling visual experiences that bridge the gap between design and technology.

With over 8 years of experience in the digital design industry, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and Fortune 500 companies to bring their visual identities to life through motion and interactive experiences.

My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision, creating work that not only looks stunning but also serves a clear purpose. Whether it's a brand identity system, an interactive website, or a motion graphics piece, I believe that every project should tell a story and create an emotional connection with its audience.
